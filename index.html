Your Free Hub for Engineering Projects, Resources, and Learning

Studgine was built with one mission: to make quality engineering education completely free and accessible for every student. Whether you are a first-year looking for beginner electronics projects or a final-year student searching for an advanced capstone idea, you will find everything you need right here — no sign-up, no paywall, no cost.

141+ Documented Engineering Projects

Our engineering projects library covers the full spectrum from simple LED circuits to industrial IoT systems and machine-learning-powered robots. Every project includes a complete components list, estimated budget, step-by-step build instructions, and source code. Projects are tagged by difficulty — beginner, intermediate, and advanced — so you can instantly filter by what matches your skill level. Popular categories include Arduino project ideas for engineering students, ESP32 IoT builds, Raspberry Pi computer vision systems, robotics, FPGA design, and renewable energy projects.

Budget Electronics Buying Guides

Tight on budget? Our budget electronics guide compares the best microcontrollers, sensors, and modules available for engineering students. Find the cheapest Arduino, ESP32, and Raspberry Pi deals without sacrificing quality. We break down price-to-performance ratios so you can make informed buying decisions and stretch every rupee or dollar further.

Scholarships, Free Books, and Tools

Beyond hardware, Studgine's resources section curates 20+ engineering scholarships and fellowships updated regularly, a library of free engineering textbooks and PDFs, online circuit simulation tools, calculators, and career development guides. Whether you need funding for your studies or free reference material for your next exam, the resources hub has you covered.

Free Engineering Learning Platform — Studgine Learn

Studgine Learn is our in-browser learning platform where engineering students can study structured subject notes, practise past-exam questions, and explore topic deep-dives — all without leaving the site. Currently covering Computer Science Engineering topics, with more departments being added continuously. Visit Studgine Learn to start studying today.
