👤 About Us

Student-Built for Engineering Students

Studgine was created by engineering students who were tired of scattered, paywalled, or outdated resources. We built the platform we wished we had.

Our Mission

To make quality engineering education and resources accessible to every student, anywhere in the world — completely free.

Our Story

Studgine started as a simple collection of project notes shared between engineering classmates. We kept seeing the same problems — students wasting time hunting across dozens of sites for project ideas, budget component guides, and scholarship lists, only to hit paywalls or find outdated information.

So we built one place for everything. Every project on Studgine is written by engineering students for engineering students — with real component costs, working code, and honest explanations of how things work (and sometimes why they don’t).

“Engineering education should be open. Every student deserves access to good resources, regardless of where they are or how much money they have.”

What Makes Studgine Different

We obsess over the small things that matter: real budget estimates, beginner-friendly explanations alongside advanced content, and projects that actually work when you build them. No filler, no fluff.

Every project page includes a component list with realistic costs, full working code, a step-by-step build guide, and extension ideas so you can take it further. The electronics buying guide saves you from overspending on parts you don’t need.

Open & Growing

Studgine is a living platform. We add new projects, update existing ones, and expand the scholarship and resources lists regularly. If you spot an error or want to contribute, reach out — we’re always looking for fellow engineers who want to give back.
