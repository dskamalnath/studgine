Budget Electronics Guide

Honest price comparisons and buying advice for popular engineering boards and components — so you spend smart and build more.

Microcontrollers

Development Boards

The most popular boards for engineering student projects.

🆕

Arduino Uno R3

Best for: Beginners
$4 – $8 (clone)  |  $25 (official)

The classic 8-bit AVR board. Huge community, tons of tutorials, 5V tolerant. Perfect for first projects.

Pros

  • Massive tutorial library
  • Rugged 5V I/O
  • Easy USB upload

Cons

  • Slow 16MHz clock
  • 2KB RAM only
  • No Wi-Fi/BT built-in
📡

ESP32 DevKit

Best for: IoT & Wi-Fi Projects
$3 – $6 (clone)  |  $10 (official)

Dual-core 240MHz, built-in Wi-Fi + Bluetooth, 520KB RAM. Best value board for IoT and wireless projects.

Pros

  • Wi-Fi + Bluetooth built-in
  • Dual core 240MHz
  • Dirt cheap

Cons

  • 3.3V I/O (not 5V tolerant)
  • No hardware USB (most clones)
📸

Raspberry Pi 4B

Best for: ML & Linux Projects
$35 (2GB) – $75 (8GB)

Quad-core ARM Cortex-A72, runs full Linux. Best for computer vision, ML inference, MQTT brokers, and Node-RED.

Pros

  • Full Linux OS
  • USB 3.0 + Gigabit Ethernet
  • GPIO + Camera port

Cons

  • Pricey
  • Needs SD card + PSU
  • No ADC pins
🔨

Arduino Nano

Best for: Compact builds
$2 – $4 (clone)

Same ATmega328P as Uno but breadboard-friendly. Perfect when space is tight and you need lots of analog pins.

Pros

  • 8 analog inputs
  • Breadboard-friendly
  • Very cheap

Cons

  • Mini-USB (older clones)
  • Same limits as Uno
Sensors & Modules

Must-Have Components

Affordable sensors and modules for engineering projects.

🌡

DHT22 Temperature & Humidity

~$2

Digital temp + humidity sensor. ±0.5°C accuracy. Single-wire protocol. Works with Arduino, ESP32, and Pi.

🔈

HC-SR04 Ultrasonic

~$1

2cm – 400cm range ultrasonic distance sensor. Simple TRIG/ECHO interface. Essential for obstacle avoidance robots.

📺

ESP32-CAM

~$5

ESP32 + OV2640 2MP camera + micro SD slot. Perfect for security cameras, face detection, and live video streaming.

🚪

L298N Motor Driver

~$1.5

Dual H-bridge driver for DC and stepper motors. 2A per channel, 5V–35V supply. The go-to module for robotics projects.

🎯

Buy Smart

Always buy generic/clone boards from AliExpress or Amazon for prototyping. Save official boards for final products.

📦

Starter Kit

A good starter kit: Arduino Uno clone + breadboard + jumpers + 10-pack LEDs + resistor kit — under $10 total.

💡

Pro Tip

Order components 2–3 weeks before your project deadline. Budget for 20% extra parts for mistakes and replacements.
